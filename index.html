ESTIMATE YOUR FINISH TIME

Estimate how long will it take you to finish your favorite race.

FinishTime <- function (distance, pace) {
  time <- as.numeric(pace)*as.numeric(distance)/60
  s<-as.character(round(((time%%1*60)%%1)*60));
  m<-as.character((time%%1*60)%/%1)
  h<-as.character(time%/%1)
  paste(h,"hour(s)",m,"minute(s)",s, "second(s)",sep=" ")}

Say you want to run half a marathon at a pace of 4:30 min/km.

FinishTime(21.1,4.5)

## [1] "1 hour(s) 34 minute(s) 57 second(s)"

ESTIMATE YOUR ENERGY CONSUMPTION

Find out how many calories will you need to recover your energy:

Calories <- function (distance, pace) {
  time <- as.numeric(pace)*as.numeric(distance)/60
  calories <- as.character(round(((9-as.numeric(pace))/(9-2.25))*1500*time))}

After your previous effort we bet you can go for a burger ;-)

Calories(21.1,4.5)

## [1] 1582

COMPARE TO OTHER RUNNERS!

Find out how fast you are and which percentile are you in!

Percentile <- function (speed){
  percentile <- as.character(round(pnorm(speed,mean=12,sd=3)*100)); print(percentile)}

Percentile(c(9,12,15,18))

## [1] "16" "50" "84" "98"
